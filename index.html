Overview

My name is Deriv Diggs and I'm the founder and chief engineer of Thought Render. I've been creating websites, RIAs, kiosks, desktop applications, games, and mobile applications using Adobe AIR/Flash/Flex/Flash Builder/HTML5/CSS3/Javascript. Twelve years ago, before focusing on development, I was a senior designer at Grey Interactive (at the time the interactive division of Grey Advertising) in New York City. Therefore, in addition to my skills as a programmer, I'm extremely proficient with the entire Adobe CS suite of programs especially Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash and Dreamweaver. I won the Adobe Site of the Day award for April 11, 2009 for:

  • http://www.sawayasegalas.com
  • http://www.sawayasegalas.com/about_sawaya_segalas/executive_bios.aspx

My extensive knowlege of both Flash and Jquery/Javascript, gives me the ablitiy to turn your Flash projects into html5 without loosing effects or functionality. I can also use Flash to change your web applications into native applications for the desktop and for mobile devices.

In addition to front end technologies, I have experience programming php, Python, and mySQL, and have extensive experience building dynamic projects that get their content from a variety of backend technologies. Please find my resume, portfolio, and contact information by scrolling down or using the menu at the top.
